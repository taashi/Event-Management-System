What's this all about?

The College Football Playoffs determine who is this year's college football national champion. The playoffs consist of two semifinal games: The Capital One Orange Bowl and The Goodyear Cotton Bowl. The winners of those two games then meet one week later in the national championship game.

Event dates and times

  • Capital One Orange Bowl, 29 December 2018, 2pm EST
  • Goodyear Cotton Bowl, 29 December 2018, 5pm EST
  • National Championship, 7 January 2019, 8pm EST
